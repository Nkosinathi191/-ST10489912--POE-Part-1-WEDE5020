Our Story

Trivest Publishing was founded in 2019.

The house aims to bring attention to different voices that detail real human experiences.

It hopes to publish stories written by young people.

Our Values

    Mission: To publish stories written by young people of colour that moves people.
    Vision: To become a leading publishing house for young indie authors.

Trivest Publishing House

Discovering powerful voices and bringing compelling stories to readers across South Africa and the world.
