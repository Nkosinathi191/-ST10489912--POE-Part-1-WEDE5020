Our Story

Founded in 2019 by a Nomsa Dlamini in Pretoria, Trivest Publishing House was born out of Nomsa’s dream: to create a platform for young voices often overlooked in mainstream publishing. What started as a small independent press has grown into a publishing house reaching readers across South Africa and beyond. Our commitment is to bring fresh, bold, and diverse stories to life.

Our Values

    Mission: To publish powerful stories written by young people of colour that move, inspire, and challenge readers.
    Vision: To become a leading publishing house for emerging indie authors in Africa and worldwide.

Our Impact

Since our founding, we’ve proudly: Published over 30 debut and established authors. Reached readers in 10+ countries through digital and print distribution. Seen our authors nominated for national and international literary awards.
